Some Projects I've Made

Haul Away!

Haul Away! is the final year project for my course at Singapore Polytechnic.
It is a chaotic couch cooperative delivery game for 2-4 players.

Set in the hectic high seas of Baboom Bay and hired by Shady Steve, gather your friends and coordinate as a crew to deliver goods before time runs out!
Take care to not lose your cargo to the dangers on the high seas!

SpaceRunners

A competitive multiplayer networking PC game school project developed during Year 3 of my Polytechnic studies, from October to December 2020.

A 3D platformer featuring asymmetric gameplay where one player plays as a space overlord and the others play as space runners. The overlords' goal is to destroy all the runners while the runners goal is to collect objectives on the map.

A single player, cube puzzle game created for a school project.

The player plays as a sticky cube- each plate on the cube will attach to an empty space in the grid-filled world. Step onto an empty space without a plate on your bottom face and you will fall through the grid and lose.
